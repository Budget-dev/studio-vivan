# **App Name**: Vivaan Farms

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Filtering: Browse a comprehensive catalog of A2 Ghee and farm goods, with the ability to filter products by category ('ghee', 'oils', 'combos', 'superfoods') and view product cards featuring essential information and interactive elements. Search products across categories.
- Detailed Product View: View extensive product information, including high-fidelity illustrations, variants (e.g., volume/size), customer ratings, stock levels, and detailed descriptions within a modal interface.
- Shopping Cart Management: Add products to a persistent shopping cart, adjust quantities, and remove items via an off-canvas sidebar, providing real-time updates on selected products.
- Checkout and Payment Simulation: Review order summaries, apply coupon codes, and proceed through a simulated payment workflow with various payment method options leading to an order confirmation.
- Order Success Confirmation: Display a success modal upon completing a simulated purchase, showing order details, earned 'Purity Coins', and delivery information.
- Live Purchase Notification Tool: An AI tool that dynamically generates and displays simulated live purchase notifications at intervals to encourage user engagement and create social proof for popular products.
- Wishlist Functionality: Add or remove products from a personal wishlist, accessible from the main navigation, for future purchase consideration.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, rich forest green (#0A2E18) to convey nature, purity, and heritage. It forms the backbone of the branding and high-contrast elements.
- Background color: A soft, muted cream/beige (#F8F6F0), offering a warm and inviting canvas that reflects natural and artisanal qualities.
- Accent color: A vibrant goldenrod yellow (#FDE88A) used for calls-to-action, highlights, and elements demanding attention, symbolizing the richness and premium quality of ghee.
- Headline font: 'Cormorant Garamond' (serif) for an elegant, literary, and traditional feel, used for titles and brand elements. Body font: 'Jost' (sans-serif) for a modern, clean, and highly readable experience across all general text.
- Utilize a blend of clean, minimalist SVG line icons for functional elements (search, cart, user) and illustrative, simple SVGs for product representations and brand trust indicators. Emojis are used for playful badging and quick informational cues.
- A responsive layout featuring a full-width hero section with inner content containers for focused information. Content is organized into clean grid patterns for product listings, trust benefits, and testimonials. Navigation includes a sticky header, an off-canvas cart sidebar, and modals for detailed product views and payment flows.
- Subtle, fluid animations enhance interactivity, including scroll-reveal effects for sections, smooth transitions for product cards on hover, and gentle float/spin animations for visual accents. Dynamic animations like ticker scrolls, button hover effects, and confetti on successful orders add delight and engagement.
